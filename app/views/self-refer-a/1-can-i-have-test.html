Check if you can have a coronavirus test

You can only apply for this test if you are a key worker who is self-isolating (not able to leave home) because:

  • you have coronavirus symptoms
  • you live with someone who has coronavirus symptoms

You should not apply for a test if you are not a key worker.

Am I a key worker?

Key workers are people providing an essential service during the coronavirus pandemic. Examples are NHS staff, teachers, and public transport staff.

Check the full list of key workers on GOV.UK
